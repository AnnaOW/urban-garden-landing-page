Urban Garden - what is it?

Urban garden is a green oasis cultivated in an urban setting.
üå± There are various types of urban gardens, e.g.:

  • Container gardening: suitable for small patios, yards, or balconies. It uses diffrent containers, e.g. buckets, old tires, barrels, etc. to grow plants.
  • Community gardening: utilising outdoor public or private spaces to cultivate gardens as a group.
  • Green roofs: roofs designed for the aim of cultivating plants.

Preventing the urban heat island

Urban areas are generally hotter than their surroundings. Plant vegetation helps to cool off the air.

Eating healthier

Fresher fruits & vegetables are consumed locally. They don't contain pesticides & supplements needed in commercial farming.

Building the community

It is a common space for all to grow their own plants & create a sense of community. It is a common ground for social gatherings for diverse groups of city inhabitans.

Relieving stress

Urban gardens are a form of leisure, they provide recreational spaces for urban dwellers. Enjoying the nature has the power to decerease stress levels.
